# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
A mobile game with strategic elements.

## Vision
To create an engaging and immersive mobile strategy game that challenges players with tactical decision-making and resource management, fostering a vibrant community around competitive and cooperative gameplay.

## Target Audience
Mobile gamers who enjoy strategy, resource management, and competitive/cooperative multiplayer experiences. This includes players familiar with real-time strategy (RTS) or turn-based strategy (TBS) genres, as well as those looking for a deep and rewarding mobile gaming experience.

## Core Features
*   **Strategic Gameplay:** Players will manage a "horde" of units, deploying them strategically in battles against AI or other players.
*   **Resource Management:** Players will gather and manage various in-game resources to expand their horde, upgrade units, and build structures.
*   **Unit Customization & Progression:** A diverse roster of units with unique abilities, upgrade paths, and customization options.
*   **PvE and PvP Modes:** Engaging single-player campaigns and challenging multiplayer modes, including ranked ladders and cooperative challenges.
*   **Community & Social Features:** Guilds, chat, and friend systems to encourage player interaction and community building.
